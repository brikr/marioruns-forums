sup y'all,

I made a cannon shot setup for rolling out on to the cloud with the 6th and 7th coins. I guess it could be faster if you can line it up fast.



What you wanna do is somewhat line the right corner of the bottom arrow up with the designated edge of the cloud and make sure around half of the side of the cloud in the circle is showing when you shoot. The setup really isn't too strict. Like I said, I don't think this could be faster unless you line it up pretty fast (sorry VCheaters). So yeah if you're a god at aiming give it a whirl
