Hello everyone,

So, I'm here to put some on!

I became interested in speedrunning at the end of 2007.
My first game is GoldenEye 007.
I browsed some websites and I became interested in Super Mario 64, but I only did some individual levels because I actually thought I couldn't do 16 Star. After 3 years of not playing from 2010 to 2013, I finally decided to just learn 16 Star and just do races on SRL.

My first run was a 32:37 in 16 Star.
Now I have 19:05 which is pretty good.

So, I'm focussing a lot on 70 Star now.
My PB is 1:02:40 which is pretty bad. My goal is ofcourse the sub 1 so the will no laugh at me anymore

I also did a very few 120 Star runs. My first run was a 4:08:xx, which I was not even happy about because it wasn't even a sub 4.
Now I have 2:59:13 which was my 4th run.

Have fun
