How many hours have you played SM64?

by RWhiteGoose » 16 May 2014, 21:12

I have been driving this discussion a bit lately. I state that if you can become a Top 10 quality player in your speedgame in under 3000 hours, it's not a competitive speedgame. I know that SM64 is one of only a handful of actually competitive speedgames, so am I off? I could consider revising the statement to about 2000 hours, but I'm sure everyone here agrees that if you can become Top 10 in under 1000 hours your speedgame is a joke.

Toastrider claims "way less than" 3000 hours played, and Haliinen concurs. I consider Top 10 to having "Top 10 times or obviously capable of such in both 120 and 70 star" but maybe that's not a great criteria as a fair few 120ers don't care about 70?

Anyways, post estimates of your the hours you've played SM64. I especially am interested in hearing from top players.
