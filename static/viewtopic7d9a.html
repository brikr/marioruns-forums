Classic :
Press D-Pad LEFT to save current position.
Press D-Pad RIGHT to return to saved position.
Change 28208E5A to 291AXXXX for Gamecube controller.
Gamecube controller D-Pad values:
Down - 0400
Up - 0800
Left - 0200
Right - 0100
