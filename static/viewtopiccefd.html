***RANKINGS - OVERALL CONTROL OF MARIO***

by RWhiteGoose » 08 Aug 2014, 22:48

The idea behind these rankings is that a person with a better Overall Control of Mario (OCM), referred to in non statistical speak as "movement" might not have a faster time. This could be due to external factors such as bad RNG or not spending as many hours in the game. For instance, Alex Tolio and Xiah have very good movement, but don't have good 120 Star times.

To get a better understanding, you can view the Overall Control of Bond rankings for Goldeneye speedrunning here: http://forums.the-elite.net/index.php?topic=19641.0

As you can see, players ranked ahead of one another might not have better OCB or movement, but might be ranked ahead because of more time playing the game, more current strategy usage, better RNG, etc.

Now, due to a lack of an overall rankings system in SM64 speedrunning, sorting this isn't as simple as going down the list of Top 20 and giving an appropriate OCM ranking. To further complicate this, I am by no means an expert in SM64 speedrunning, so what I will do is, rank the Top 20 players according to their 120 Star times, plus a few other significant players. The OCM score will be my interpretation, with some who I've not watched, left unscored. It can be up to you guys, the community, to make a better OCM scoring around the format I show here.

So anyways, let's do this.
