Is sub 1:40 possible on VC?

by Swiffy22 » 15 Jul 2014, 01:45

Greetings everyone! I used to speedrun Mario 64 back almost 5 years ago, when the world record was still 2:09:xx. I managed to pull a 2:42:xx a few times before I finally, slowly began to put the game down. Well, five years later, I'm back again, and really considering giving it another go. There's a lot of new strats, a lot of new faces, and plenty of new things to tackle; it all feels a bit overwhelming when I think about it.

I used to run on VC, which will be what I'll be trying this time around. I know that VC is supposed to be a minute and 30 seconds faster than N64, but will it be enough with the current strats? I remember someone in puncayshun's chat saying that the sum of his best splits added up to 1:41:xx, which leads me to believe that it could be humanly possible. But I assume most of you here have a better grasp on these concepts than I do. If it is possible, I'd want to make it my goal, even though VC isn't necessarily recognized,at least not in a positive light, within the community.

So, what are your thoughts?

Re: Is sub 1:40 possible on VC?

by Joeys » 15 Jul 2014, 15:26

Hi,

VC is around 3 minutes faster for 120 star. Therefore it is certainly possible to get 1:40. I believe, though, that you are underestimating the skill and dedication it takes to reach that level. It would take you thousands of hours, and it wouldn't really be realistic as a (short term) goal.

Unless you're willing to put in 8 hours a day for the next year, don't think about getting 1:40. Think about getting sub 2 first maybe.
