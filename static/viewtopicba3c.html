by minikori » 01 Nov 2014, 21:57

Hi Mace, pleasure to meet you.
If you are new then emulator is really good for practice because you can utilize save states. If you swap over to N64, consider purchasing a Gameshark (version 2+); the level reset code is very helpful and that way you can practice while still experiencing the proper lag you will have in runs.
It's not really worth it to get a JP cart unless you think it's cool. The time it saves in the categories where it does save time is very minimal. Really it's just up to you (make sure to get some screws or another setup to swap the backs of the carts though, if you do).
Not sure how long you've been running but 1:10 is definitely a good start! I don't have time to watch your run now but my guess is that the best way you can improve right now is just doing runs and focusing on consistency / movement.
