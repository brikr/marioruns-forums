Weekly 70 star race on SRL

by Alaris » 23 Nov 2014, 22:05

This is an advertisement thread for a weekly 70 star race on SRL.


The tentative time for this race will be 3pm EST (EDT, depending on the time of year) every Saturday. Originally, this was another iteration of the "weekly" 120 races, but after it died off for the nth time, I thought it might be worth trying 70 star this time around.

This is for people of all skill levels, so don't be afraid to join cause you think you won't be competitive. The longetivity of these weekly races seems to hinge on momentum, so the bigger these races are each week, the more likely it'll continue the next week.

Also, we can always do a 120 race afterwards if anyone is up for it.
