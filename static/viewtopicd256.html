I was thinking it would be cool if we could adapt the spreadsheet to the site:

https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/ccc ... aUnc#gid=0

It would make single star times a bit more competitive than they are on the spreadsheet, as it would be a lot easier to compare times. There's a few problems with doing it, though. The spreadsheet is great because we can just tell people not to add times that use strats they wouldn't use in an actual run. With a leaderboard format, we couldn't really do this, as it would be confusing to people to see a #1 time on a star that isn't WR.

The much bigger problem, however, is how much work this would be. While the current drop down menu on the site is perfect for just a handful of categories, you obviously couldn't have a simple menu for 120 individual stars. So it would probably be a lot of work. Still, I think it'd be cool, as the spreadsheet is way too fucking crowded and retarded looking.
