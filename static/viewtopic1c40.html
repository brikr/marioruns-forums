by Denotu » 14 Apr 2014, 05:51

Yo,

So I recently added a module which enables users to receive coins based on their contributions towards the forum. I feel this is a great way to improve forum activity and quality of the content posted.

With this thread I hope to answer any questions regarding how the system actually works.

Basically the whole coins system is a form of forum money that you get for posting meaningful topics. The current rate is 3 coins per 1 topic started. There's also a lot of other ways you can earn coins (events, replying to threads, creating polls, etc.)

If you're feeling generous you can also donate your coins to other users, (or maybe you can use them to bet against each other in various races or Tinychat events), but I guess the uses of coins will be up to you. Oh, I'll be creating a Shop on the forum very soon (selling colored names, custom ranks, etc.) to give you guys something to show for it.

So that's pretty much it about coins for now.

Hope you enjoy.
